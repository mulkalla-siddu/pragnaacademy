Odela chandramouli

Chandramouli Odela, the visionary founder of Pragna Academy. With over 15 years of dedicated experience in the field of physics education, Chandramouli is renowned for his passion for teaching and his commitment to nurturing young minds. A seasoned educator, Chandramouli brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to Pragna Academy. His journey in education began with a deep-seated desire to inspire students and instill in them a love for learning. Throughout his career, he has earned a reputation for his innovative teaching methods, his ability to simplify complex concepts, and his unwavering dedication to his students' success. Chandramouli's approach to education goes beyond textbooks; he believes in fostering a holistic understanding of physics through hands-on experiments, real-world applications, and interactive learning experiences. His enthusiasm for physics is contagious, motivating students to explore the depths of the subject and uncover its practical applications in everyday life. As the driving force behind Pragna Academy, Chandramouli is committed to creating an educational environment where curiosity thrives, critical thinking flourishes, and every student is empowered to reach their full potential. His leadership and passion continue to shape the academic landscape at Pragna Academy, inspiring future generations of thinkers, innovators, and problem-solvers
